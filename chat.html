Pitch narrative

Funnel Agent turns funnel drift into owned recovery work.

Dashboards show what changed. Jira shows what people are doing. Confluence stores decisions. Teams carries reminders. Funnel Agent connects those systems into one operating loop: Detect → Diagnose → Assign → Summarize → Notify.

Why agent?A chatbot answers a question. Funnel Agent executes a recurring workflow across metrics, Jira, Confluence, and Teams.
Real bottleneckThe bottleneck is not seeing that a metric moved. It is turning that movement into accountable recovery work.
Workflow principleThe LLM explains. Deterministic tools compute metrics, rank impact, validate writes, and update systems of record.
